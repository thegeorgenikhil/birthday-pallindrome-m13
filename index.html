Check out if your birthdate is Palindrome.

A palindrome is a word/number which reads the same backward as forward

Let's Go

Enter your birthdate and we will tell you if your birthdate is a palindrome

This app checks your birthdate in 3 formats yyyy-mm-dd, mm-dd-yyyy and mm-dd-yy

e.g. if your birthdate is 01 Aug 1995, then app will check for 19950801, 01081995, 080195
